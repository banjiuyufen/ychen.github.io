About me

I am a first year PhD student in the EECS department at the University of California, Berkeley. I obtained my B.S. from the Department of Mathematics at Nanjing University, and my M.S. from the Department of Computer Science, University of Virginia. Previously, I was fortunate to work with Quanquan Gu at University of Virginia, and Sinno Jialin Pan at Nanyang Technological University.
