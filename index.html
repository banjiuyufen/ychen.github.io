About me

I am a graduate student in Department of Computer Science, University of Virginia. I earned my B.S. from Department of Mathematics at Nanjing University in 2015. Previously, I worked with Quanqaun Gu at University of Virginia, and Sinno Jialin Pan at Nanyang Technological University. My research interests broadly include topics in machine learning and optimization.
